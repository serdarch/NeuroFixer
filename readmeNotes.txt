NeuroFixer: Build and Support Tool for NeuroFuser


NeuroFixer is the tool to build fully attention-based 
foundation models in computer vision based on either CNN- or ViT-based
backbones based on methods of NeuroFuser. 

NeuroFixer is developed to be up to date and freed from 
the majoirty of library dependencies and external repositories
that most of the time crash or do not fit to many virtual environments 
due to version differences of environments and setup.

NeuroFixer is developed as backbone-agnostic, error-free, user-friendly, and 
easy to use without complicated dependencies.
